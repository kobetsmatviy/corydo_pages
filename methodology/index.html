Methodology

High-level outline

Signals in this project would be derived from public market data: order flow, volume, and price action. The focus is on market microstructure — e.g. trade imbalance and liquidity pressure — as research inputs, not as trading recommendations.

This is research-oriented work. Data can be noisy; past patterns do not guarantee future results. We do not make any guarantees about accuracy or outcomes.

What we do not do

  • We do not use or rely on insider information.
  • We do not promise or guarantee any particular outcome.
  • We do not provide personalized investment or trading advice.
