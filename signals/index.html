Signals

What “signals” means here

In this project, “signals” refers to a research concept: observable patterns or metrics derived from market data (e.g. order flow, volume, price action). This is for educational and research purposes only. It is not trade advice, and nothing here should be taken as a recommendation to buy or sell.

Categories of signals (plain language)

  • Momentum — short-term price and volume dynamics.
  • Liquidity shifts — changes in available liquidity and depth.
  • Trade imbalance / liquidity pressure — order flow and imbalance as research inputs.

During the pause

No real-time signals are offered while the project is paused. Any historical or descriptive content is for context only.
